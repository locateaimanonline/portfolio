Using Signal Policies

Signal is an advanced monitoring system that tracks the condition of your Apple, Android, Windows Modern/Desktop Classic, Linux and Windows Mobile managed devices through a defined criteria. SOTI MobiControl triggers alerts in the notification panel and performs automatic mitigation actions when conditions are met.

Use Signal policies to:
  • Monitor device health and status across multiple platforms. For example, identify devices with low storage to review.
  • React faster to risk conditions with policies that do not rely on device check-ins for evaluation. For example, action devices that are out of contract from the SOTI MobiControl server.
  • Enhance operational visibility by surfacing alerts in the notification panel.
  • Track a wide range of system elements, including devices, servers, profiles, users, and certificates.
  • Trigger real-time alerts when devices meet defined conditions. For example, Actioning devices that are running low on battery.
For detailed examples, see Signal Policy Examples.

To get started, use the Signal policy wizard to create and configure your policy. See Signal Policy Wizard Overview.

Notice: Starting in version 2025.0, legacy Alert rules are deprecated and automatically converted to Signal policies. Converted policies are disabled by default and must be reviewed and re-enabled. Some legacy Alerts may fail to convert due to unsupported configurations. For details, see Legacy Alert Rules Now Signal Policies.
