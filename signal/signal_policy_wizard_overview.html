Signal Policy Wizard Overview

The SOTI MobiControl Signal Policy Wizard guides administrators through a structured, five-step process to create automated monitoring and response policies for managed devices and system components.

Each step in the wizard builds on the previous one, allowing you to define what the policy monitors, how it responds, and when it runs. This guided approach helps ensure policies are predictable, efficient, and aligned with operational requirements.

The Signal Policy Wizard consists of the following steps:

  1. Create a Signal policy to define the policy name and description. See Signal Policy Wizard | Create a Policy.
  2. Define policy conditions to specify what triggers the policy. See Signal Policy Wizard | Build Policy Conditions.
  3. Configure policy settings to adjust advanced options such as device-side evaluation. See Signal Policy Wizard | Configure Policy Settings.
  4. Define policy actions to determine what happens when conditions are met. See Signal Policy Wizard | Add Policy Actions.
  5. Schedule policy execution to control when the policy is active and how often actions run. See Signal Policy Wizard | Defining Policy Schedules.

You move through each step sequentially using the wizard interface, but you can return to earlier steps at any time to refine the configuration before saving the policy.

Tip: To update an existing Signal policy, select the policy and choose Edit to open the Signal policy wizard, where you can modify conditions and actions and save your changes. See Viewing and Editing Signal Policies.
