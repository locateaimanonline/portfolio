Signal Policy Wizard | Create a Policy

Ensure you have Manage Signal Policies permissions.
This is the first of five steps in configuring a new signal policy through the Signal policy wizard. In this step, you provide a name and description for the Signal policy. For details on the remaining steps, see Signal Policy Wizard Overview.
  1. From the SOTI MobiControl main menu, go to Policies > Signal.
  2. Select New Signal Policy to open the Signal wizard.
  3. Enter a Name and Description for the Signal policy.

Select Next to go to the Conditions tab. See Signal Policy Wizard | Build Policy Conditions.
